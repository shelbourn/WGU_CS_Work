International Programs

Shelter is one of the most basic necessities in life, yet more than 1 billion people throughout the world struggle for survival in unhealthy and unsafe living conditions. The international Campus Chapters and Youth Programs (CCYP) team works with young people and Habitat for Humanity national organizations all around the world (excluding the United States). Our team is comprised of CCYP coordinators working in Habitat for Humanity International offices in Africa and the Middle East, Asia and the Pacific, Europe and Central Asia and Latin America and the Caribbean.

We seek to promote creative and responsible ways for youth to offer their skills toward Habitat for Humanity's mission of building decent and affordable houses. CCYP invites you to join other young people around the world who are serving with those in need. Through involvement in Habitat for Humanity, you have an opportunity to expand your worldview, grow personally and spiritually, develop professional skills and, most importantly, make a difference in the lives of families in need of decent shelter.
